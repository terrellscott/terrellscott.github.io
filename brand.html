Brand Info

My college teammate Rollie Smith is a basketball trainer. He calls his business RMB Training. The RMB stands for Rollie Miyagi Basketball. He likes to think of himself as a Mr. Miyagi from the Karate Kid of basketball. He made a website with Wix that needs some work. I thought this was a great opportunity for a website. It gives me an opportunity to code, brand, and help out a friend all at the same time.

Brand Idea

I played into the idea of old school karate flicks. I wanted the logo to look like the old kung-fu posters. I found a font, Gang of Three, that reminded me of the old school karate movie posters. I thought that the whole name would be too busy, so I used a simple san serif font, Avenir Book, for "Training". I also used Avenir book for the body, and Avenir Black for the H2 Elements.

I want to go in the idea of him being a basketball sensei. I want the gym to be his dojo. Rollie likes this picture because he thinks he looks like a teacher bowing to a student. I used this picture for an svg. I could not get the praying hands to look right, so I replaced it with a basketball. I highlighted the long, white beard to make him look wise.


I'm continuing to work on the branding. Here's some ideas of all the different ways that he could use these elements in social media or clothing.

Colors

I wanted variations of the colors of a basketball, so I went with carrot and charcoal. I picked linen to match the background. The background image I picked is parchment paper. Most kung-fu movies started with a scroll telling the story thus far. So I thought I would use a scroll to tell the story.
